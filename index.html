Our Story

Vesu ka Sabse
Thanda Truck 🔶

Started from a single soda truck in Vesu, Surat, Sonu Soda has become Gujarat's most beloved stop for ice-cold drinks. Every glass is made fresh, every single time — from the first squeeze of lemon to the last bubble of soda.

No fancy restaurants. No reservations. Just real desi flavours, street energy, and prices that make everyone smile. Our orange soda truck is parked in Vesu every day from 12 PM to 10 PM.
